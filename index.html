Charity service – 2025

Turn unused items into real help for people in need.

GiveAgain connects people who have good-quality items they no longer use with individuals and families who genuinely need them – clothes, furniture, electronics, books and more.

Instead of letting things gather dust or go to waste, you can give them a second life and support your local community.

How it works

The service is designed to be simple for both donors and recipients. Once information is submitted through the forms below, the charity team will manually review and match donations with requests.

Step 1

Donors list available items

People with unused but good-quality items fill in a short form with item details, condition and location. This helps the team understand what is available.

Step 2

People in need submit requests

Individuals, families or organisations who need support describe the type of items they are looking for and any specific requirements.

Step 3

We match and coordinate

The charity reviews incoming offers and requests, then contacts both sides to coordinate safe and convenient collection or delivery.

Contact & next steps

In a real charity deployment, this section would include a phone number, official email address and social media links. For this student project, it simply explains how the service would normally operate.
